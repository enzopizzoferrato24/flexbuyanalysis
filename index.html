Model Assumptions

The following parameters were used to generate the surfaces above.

Vehicle MSRP $35,000
Loan Term 66 (75) Months
Flex Period Months 1 - 36
Payment Decrement 15% or 18% (15%)
Annual Depreciation 18% (Exp. Decay)
Step-Up Month Month 37

Note on Methodology: Equity is calculated as (Current Vehicle Value - Remaining Loan Balance). Depreciation is modeled exponentially, assuming the vehicle loses 18% of its remaining value every 12 months. The Payment Decrement is applied to the standard 66-month level-payment equivalent.
